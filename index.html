About Me

Software Engineer with a degree from the University of Brasília, focused on full-stack development and cloud infrastructure. I worked as a Full Stack Engineer at External Secrets Inc., building backend services with Python and Go, Kubernetes controllers, and web UIs with React and TypeScript. I also worked at ANTAQ, where I developed internal systems. I’ve participated in several Programming Marathons, many Game Jams, and even tried building an MMO with some friends (yeah, a bit of a crazy idea… we definitely bit off more than we could chew, but who knows, maybe one day it’ll happen?).

Ever since I can remember, I’ve been passionate about games, whether on PC, console, or mobile, and I’ve always admired the influence they have on players and the emotions they spark. My goal is to one day create games that bring this same passion to others, games that captivate players just as much as I was when I played The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time for the first time.
